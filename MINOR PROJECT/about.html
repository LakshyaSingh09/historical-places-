ABOUT INDIA

India, officially known as the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous country in the world, with over 1.3 billion people. India is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and centuries-old traditions.

The country is characterized by its vast plains, towering mountains, lush forests, and long coastline. India has a rich history that spans thousands of years, with numerous civilizations, empires, and dynasties leaving their mark on its landscape.

Famous Historical Places in India

  • Hampi
  • Amer Fort
  • Khajuraho Temples
